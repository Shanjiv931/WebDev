Maya Hernandez

Contemporary Impressionist

Maya Hernandez has been creating breathtaking impressionist pieces for over 15 years. Her work has been featured in galleries across Europe and North America, with her distinctive style blending traditional techniques with contemporary themes.

Based in Barcelona, Maya draws inspiration from urban landscapes and the interplay of light in metropolitan environments.
